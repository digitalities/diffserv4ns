### Scenario-identity finding

The folder-name collision on `example-3` masked a substantive scenario difference. The **2006 `example-3`** README describes it as *"a DS-RED script that uses a VIDEO trace for EF traffic, CBR traffic for BE traffic and the srTCM policer"* — a short 5-source demo. The **2026 `example-3`** was a full reconstruction of thesis Scenario 3 §4.3: a 771-node service-model simulation with Premium/Gold/Silver/Bronze/BE classes, LLQ scheduler, RealAudio CDFs, Appendix C validation. They are not the same scenario; they just shared the folder name.

Resolution: the public release honours the 2006 folder-name provenance (2006 `example-3` wins). The 2026 thesis §4.3 reconstruction is preserved in this dev repository under `archived/example-3-thesis-reconstruction-2026-04/` with an explanatory `ARCHIVE-README.md`, and is independently permanent on Software Heritage under the pre-rewrite SWHID `swh:1:rev:c0d23bb78711776d716760575b8db57959925298` (and at paths under `examples/example-3/` in that revision).
